[[Maximal ideal]]
# A maximal ideal in a commutative ring is prime

Let $R$ be a [[commutative ring]] and $I \triangleleft R$ be a [[maximal ideal]].
Then $I$ is a [[prime ideal]]. #m/thm/ring
