# Infinitesimal Calculus MOC
Calculus, specifically infinitesimal calculus, is the mathematical study of continuous change.
Infinitesimal calculus is traditionally broken into two branches:
[[#Differential Calculus]] and [[#Integral Calculus]];
and these are linked together by the [[Fundamental theorem of calculus]].
In education, Calculus forms the basic foundations of further [[Mathematics MOC#Analysis|analysis]].
