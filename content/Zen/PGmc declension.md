# PGmc declension

## Nouns

The declension of nouns varied depending on the stem class.
The distribution of PGmc noun stem classes and gender was as follows[^rin]

[^rin]: 2017, [[@ringeProtoIndoEuropeanProtoGermanic2017|From Proto-Indo-European to Proto-Germanic]], §4.3.4, p. 299

- [[PGmc a-stems]] < [[PIE o-stems]]: masculine, neuter;
- [[PGmc ō-stems]] < [[PIE ah₂-stems]]: feminine;
- [[PGmc ī~jō-stems]] < [[PIE ih₂~yah₂-stems]]: feminine;
- [[PGmc i-stems]]: all three genders (few neuters);
- [[PGmc u-stems]]: all three genders (few neuters, fairly few feminines);
- [[PGmc n-stems]]: all three genders (few neuters);
- [[PGmc r~n stems]]: two neuters;
- [[PGmc r-stems]]: two masculine, three feminine (kinship terms);
- [[PGmc z-stems]]: neuter;
- [[PGmc miscellaneous stems]]: all three genders (few neuters).
