# Artin braid group

The **Artin braid group** $\mathfrak{B}_{n}$ on $n$ strands is the special case of the more general [[braid group]] on the [[Euclidean plane]] $\mathbb{R}^2$:
$$
\begin{align*}
\mathfrak{B}_{n} = \mathfrak{B}_{n}(\mathbb{R}^2) = \pi_{1} {\mathbb{R}^2 \choose n}.
\end{align*}
$$
It has [[Group presentation|presentation]] $\langle \sigma_{1} , \dots, \sigma_{{n-1}} : R \rangle$ #m/thm/topology 
where $R$ consists of the relations 
$$
\begin{align*}
\sigma_{i}\sigma_{j}\sigma_{i} &= \sigma_{j}\sigma_{i}\sigma_{j} & \abs{i-j} &= 1, \\
\sigma_{i}\sigma_{j} &= \sigma_{j}\sigma_{i} & \abs{i-j} &\neq 1.
\end{align*}
$$
