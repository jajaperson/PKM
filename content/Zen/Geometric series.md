[[Infinite series]]
# Geometric series
The geometric series are a family of series of the form
$$
\begin{align*}
\sum_{n=0}^\infty r^n
\end{align*}
$$
such series converge if and only if $\abs{r} < 1$, 
and in these cases the result is determined to be
$$
\begin{align*}
s = \sum_{n=0}^\infty r^n = \frac{1}{1-r}.
\end{align*}
$$
This follows from the partial sum (for $r\neq {1}$) is given by
$$
\begin{align*}
s_{k} = \sum_{n=0}^k r^n = \frac{1-r^{k+1}}{1-r}
\end{align*}
$$
