# Proof system

A **proof system** or **proof calculus** is a [[formal system]] used to prove statements.

## à la Hilbert

Proof systems à la Hilbert leave little distinction between propositions and judgements.

## à la Gentzen

Proof systems à la Gentzen are distinguished by their use of [[Context in a proof system|contexts]],
so that every line in a deduction (i.e. every judgement) is conditional on zero or more assumptions,
having the form
$$
\begin{align*}
\Gamma \vdash \mathcal{J}.
\end{align*}
$$
Examples of such systems include

- [[Natural deduction]]
- [[Sequent calculus]]
- [[Type theory|Type theories]]
