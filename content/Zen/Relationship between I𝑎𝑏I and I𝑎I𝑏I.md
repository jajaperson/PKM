# Relationship between $\abs{ab}$ and $\abs a \abs b$

Given a finite group $G$, and elements $a,b \in G$ such that $ab = ba$,
then $\abs{ab}$ divides $\abs a \abs b$. #m/thm/group

> Let $\abs{a} = m$ and $\abs b = n$.
