# Division of NGmc languages


A rough timeline for the division of North Germanic languages is as follows[^hau]

- Before ca. ==1050== the main division was between ==East Nordic and West Nordic==
- From ca. ==1050== to ==1350== the main division is between ==North Nordic and South Nordic== <!--SR:!2000-01-01,1,250!2000-01-01,1,250!2000-01-01,1,250!2024-01-27,1,210!2000-01-01,1,250-->
