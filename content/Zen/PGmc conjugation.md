# PGmc conjugation

As with nouns, most verbs belonged to one of several large inflectional classes.[^ringe]

1. [[PGmc strong verbs]]
3. [[PGmc weak verbs]]
4. [[PGmc preterite-presents]]
5. [[PGmc anomolous verbs]]

[^ringe]: 2017, [[@ringeProtoIndoEuropeanProtoGermanic2017|From Proto-Indo-European to Proto-Germanic]], pp. 262–263

## Ringe's classification

The lexical classes are classified by Ringe as

- I. [[PGmc strong verbs]]
  - A. [[PGmc unaffixed thematic presents]]
  - B. [[PGmc presents in *-i- ~ *-ja-]]
  - C. [[PGmc thematic presents with a nasal affix]]
- II. [[PGmc weak verbs]]
  - A. [[PGmc *bringaną-class]] with unsuffixed thematic present, past with no vowel before the suffix.
  - B. [[PGmc *wurkijaną-class]] with presents in <em class="recon">-i-</em> ~ <em class="recon">-ja-</em>, past with no vowel before the suffix (subclass of weak I)
  - C. [[PGmc *lagjaną-class]] with presents in <em class="recon">-i-</em> ~ <em class="recon">-ja-</em>, past with <em class="recon">-i-</em> before the suffix (main weak I)
  - D. [[PGmc *karō̄ną-class]] with presents in <em class="recon">-ō̄-</em>, past with <em class="recon">-ō-</em> before the suffix (weak II)
  - E. [[PGmc *sagjaną-class]] with presents in <em class="recon">-ai-</em> ~ <em class="recon">-ja-</em>, past with no vowel before the suffix (subclass of weak III)
  - F. [[PGmc *armāną-class]] with presents in <em class="recon">-ai-</em> ~ <em class="recon">-ā-</em>, past possibly with <em class="recon">-a-</em> before the suffix (main weak III)
  - G.[[PGmc *þurznaną-class]] with presents in <em class="recon">-nō-</em> ~ <em class="recon">-na-</em>, past probably with <em class="recon">-no-</em> before the suffix (weak IV)
- III. [[PGmc preterite-presents]]
- IV. [[PGmc anomolous verbs]]
