# Compactification

A **compactification** is a process which makes a [[topological space]] into a [[Compact space|compact space]].
Intuitively, this means “adding points at infinity”.

## Compactification

- If $X$ is a [[Locally compact space]], its [[Alexandrov extension]] $X^\star$ is compact.
