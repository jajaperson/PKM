# Nonlinear centres with conserved quantities

Consider the [[differential system]] $\dot{x}=f(x)$ with [[Differentiability|differentiable]] $f :\mathbb{R}^n \to \mathbb{R}^n$.
Suppose there exists a [[conserved quantity]] $E(x)$
and that $x^*$ is an isolated fixed point.
If $x^*$ is a [[local extremum]] of $E$, then there exists a neighbourhood of $x^*$ containing only orbits.[^2024]
