# Rational numbers

The **rational numbers** $\mathbb{Q}$ are the [[field of fractions]] of the [[integers]], #m/def/gen
and are thus, in many ways, the most general [[field]].

## Properties

1. $x \in \mathbb{Q}$ is [[Integral element|integral]] over [[Integers]] iff $x \in \mathbb{Z}$, i.e. the [[Algebraic integer|algebraic integers]] of $\mathbb{Q}$ are precisely $\mathbb{Z}$. ^P1
