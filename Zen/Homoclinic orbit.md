[[Differential system]]
# Homoclinic orbit

A **homoclinic orbit** is a [[trajectory]] which loops around on itself in infinite time,
i.e. if $x$ lies in such an orbit $\lim_{ t \to \infty }\Phi^tx = \lim_{ t \to -\infty } = x^*$,
where $x^*$ is necessarily a saddle node called a **homoclinic point**.

Homoclinic orbits are likely to appear in a system with a [[Conserved quantity]].
In contrast a [[Heteroclinic trajectory]] flows from one saddle to another.
