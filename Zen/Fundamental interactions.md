# Interaction
In particle physics, an **interaction** is any event that involves an exchange of _momentum_ and/or _energy_ between particles.
There are four **fundamental interactions**,
each mediated by a different [[Virtual particle|virtual boson]].


| Name                            | Mediating boson      | Effect                                                                                         | Residual form                                                  | Effective range |
| ------------------------------- | -------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------- |
| [[Strong interaction]]          | gluon                | Binds [[Quark\|quarks]] into [[Classification of particles under the Standard Model\|hadrons]] | Binds nucleons into nuclei                                     | Short           |
| [[Electromagnetic interaction]] | photon               | Binds electrons and nuclei in atoms                                                            | Binds atoms into molecules, molecules into objects like chairs | Medium          |
| [[Gravitational interaction]]   | graviton[^hyp]       | Binds matter into planets, stars, galaxies, &c.                                                | -                                                              | Long            |
| [[Weak interaction]]            | $\ce{ Z^0 , W+, W-}$ | [[Beta decay]]                                                                                 | -                                                              | Short           |

[^hyp]: Theorized
