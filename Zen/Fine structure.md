# Fine structure

**Fine structure** refers to the energy level splitting caused by a number of corrections to the [[Coulomb potential]] which are of the order of a milliëlectronvolt, transitions wherebetween produce radiation in the far-infrared range.
The corrections are due to the effects of [[Spin-orbit coupling]] and relativistic corrections.

When discussing fine structure it is conventional to use [[Russel-Saunders term symbol|Russel-Saunders term symbols]], even those these are only really useful in the regime of [[LS-coupling]] (See [[Spin-orbit coupling]]).
