[[Completeness and cocompleteness]]
# Finite (co)completeness criterion

A category $\cat C$ is [[Completeness and cocompleteness|finitely (co)complete]] iff #m/thm/cat 

1. it has binary [[Products and coproducts|(co)products]] and [[Equalizer and coëqualizer|(co)equalizers]].
2. it has [[Fibre product and coproduct|fibre (co)products]] and a [[Initial and terminal objects|terminal (initial) object]].
