# Pitchfork bifurcation

## Flows

The **pitchfork bifurcation** comes in two forms, the **supercritical pitchfork bifurcation**
$$
\begin{align*}
\dot{x} = \mu x-x^3
\end{align*}
$$
where a stable fixed point ejects a pair of stable fixed points and become unstable;
and the **subcritical pitch fork bifurcation**
$$
\begin{align*}
\dot{x}=\mu x+x^3
\end{align*}
$$
where two unstable fixed points collide with a stable fixed point producing a single unstable fixed point.
A rotational version is the [[Hopf bifurcation]]
