# Bifurcation

A **bifurcation** occurs when a smooth variation of the parameters of a [[Dynamical system]] results in a sudden qualitative change in behaviour.

## Local bifurcations

Local bifurcations occur at non-hyperbolic fixed points and change the number of fixed points.[^2021]
The **codimension** refers to the number of parameters.

[^2021]: 2021\. [[Sources/@walkerMATH3021NonlinearDynamics2021|MATH3021: Nonlinear dynamics & chaos]], p. 72

- 1D
  - [[Saddle-node bifurcation]] $\dot{x} = \mu - x^2$
  - [[Transcritical bifurcation]] $\dot{x}=\mu x - x^2$
  - [[Pitchfork bifurcation]] $\dot{x}=\mu x-x^3$
- 2D
  - [[Hopf bifurcation]] $\dot{r}=\alpha r-r^3$ and $\dot{\omega}=1$
