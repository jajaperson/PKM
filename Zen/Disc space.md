# Disc space

The <dfn>$n$-disc</dfn> $\mathbb{D}^n$ is usually defined as the closed unit-[[Open ball|ball]] in [[Real coördinate space|$\mathbb R^n$]], i.e.
$$
\begin{align*}
\mathbb{D}^n = \{ v \in \mathbb{R}^n : \|v\| \leq 1 \} = \overline{\opn{B}_{1}(0)} \sube \mathbb{R}^n
\end{align*}
$$
along with the subspace metric and [[subspace topology]].
Its boundary is the $(n-1)$-[[sphere space]].

## Properties

- The $n$-disc is a [[Compact space]].
- [[Brouwer's fixed point theorem]]
- [[Brouwer's fixed point theorem#Corollaries|Retraction theorem]]
