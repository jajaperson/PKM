# Metrics on spacetime
There are three principal metrics in flat (i.e. [[Minkowski spacetime|Minkowski]]) spacetime.
The first two are variant depending on observer (the reference frame):

1. **Space interval** — The distance between two points is given by the well known pythagorean theorem $\sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2 + (z_2 - z_1)^2}$.
2. **Time interval*** — The time between points is given by $t_2 - t_1$.

The invariant metric on is the **spacetime interval**:

3. **[[Spacetime interval]]** — Given by $(\Delta s)^2 = (\Delta d)^2 - (\Delta t)^2$
