[[R-algebra]]
# Associative algebra over a commutative ring

Let $\mathcal{K}$ be a [[commutative ring]].
An **$\mathcal{K}$-semigroup** $T$ can be viewed in two equivalent ways: #m/def/ring 
1. As an $\mathcal{K}$-[[R-algebra|algebra]] which is associative;
2. As a [[rng]] $T$ equipped with an $\mathcal{K}$-action.

It is thus a [[Semigroup object]] in [[Category of modules over a commutative ring]]
