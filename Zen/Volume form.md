# Volume form

Let $M$ be an $n$-dimensional $C^\alpha$-[[manifold]].
A **volume form** $\dj V \in \Omega^n(M)$ is simply a choice of a [[Differential form|top form]], #m/def/geo/diff 
i.e. a differential form of rank $n$.
In certain cases there exists a canonical volume form

- [[Riemannian volume form]] for an oriented semi-Riemannian manifold.

## Properties

1. A nonvanishing volume form $\dj V$ induces an orientation on $M$. ^P1
2. $M$ is [[Oriented manifold|orientable]] iff it admits a nonvanishing volume form $\dj V$. ^P2
