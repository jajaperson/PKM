# Reversible 2D flow

A [[Differential system]] 
$$
\begin{align*}
\dot{x} &= f(x,y) \\
\dot{y} &= g(x,y)
\end{align*}
$$
is **reversible** iff it is invariant under the transformation $t \to -t$, $y \to -y$, #m/def/dynamics/flow 
i.e.
$$
\begin{align*}
-f(x,y) &= f(x, - y) \\
g(x,y) &= g(x,-y) 
\end{align*}
$$
which is to say $f$ is [[Odd and even functions|odd]] w.r.t $y$ and $g$ is [[Odd and even functions|even]].
