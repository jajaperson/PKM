# Quantum time evolution operator

The [[time evolution operator]] for a quantum system in the [[Schrödinger picture]] with a time-independent [[Hamiltonian operator]] $\hat{H}$ is given by the [[matrix exponential]]
$$
\begin{align*}
U^t = e^{-i t \hat{H}/\hbar}
\end{align*}
$$
Since $\hat{H}$ is a [[Hermitian operator]] it follows from [[Hermitian operator#^P1]] that $U^t$ is [[Unitary operator|unitary]].
