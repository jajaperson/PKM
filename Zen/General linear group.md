# General linear group

The **general linear group** $\mathrm{GL}(V)$
is the [[Automorphism|automorphism group]] of the vector space $V$ in [[Category of vector spaces]].
When dealing with matrices, the notation $\mathrm{GL}_{n}(\mathbb{K})$ refers to invertible $n \times n$ [[Matrix algebra over a field|matrices]] over the field $\mathbb{K}$.
Of particular importance is the [[Complex general linear group]] and its subgroup [[Real general linear group]].

## Properties

- [[Matrix determinant is a homomorphism]]


## Related groups

- A subgroup is the [[Special linear group]], which can be defined as the [[Kernel of a group homomorphism]] of the [[Matrix determinant]].
- [[Centre of the general linear group]] consists of scalar matrices and is isomorphic to the multiplicative group $\mathbb{K}^\times$.
