# Category of categories

A **category of categories** is a [[category]] $\cat C$ such that any two objects $\cat A, \cat B \in \Ob \cat C$ are categories
and the morphisms $\cat A \to \cat B$ are exactly the [[functor|functors]] $\cat A \to \cat B$. #m/def/cat/evil 

Note that in general, equality of functors is ill-defined unless we have equality of objects.
Therefore categories of categories are usually restricted to [[strict category|strict categories]], see e.g. [[Category of strict categories]].
More general categories (without equality of objects) assemble into bicategories, see e.g. [[Bicategory of categories]]
