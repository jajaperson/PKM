# Real numbers
The **real numbers** are a _completion_ of [[Rational numbers]] using the axiom

> Every non-empty [[Bounded set|bounded above]] subset of $\mathbb{R}$ has a [[Poset#^sup|supremum]]

which also implies that every non-empty bounded below subset of $\mathbb{R}$ has an [[Poset#^inf|infimum]].

See also [[Real coördinate space]].
