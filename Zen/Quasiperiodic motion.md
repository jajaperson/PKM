# Quasiperiodic motion

**Quasiperiodic motion** of order $k$ is the result of dynamics with $k$ periodic components with [[Rational independence|rationally independent]] frequencies.[^2022] #m/def/dynamics 
While a quasiperiodic trajectory will never repeat itself,
this kind of motion in qualitatively different to [[Chaos]]. (why? #to/elaborate)
The natural manifold for $k$th order quasiperiodic dynamics is the $k$-[[Torus topology|torus]].
Thus for euclidean manifolds the minimum dimension for quasiperiodic dynamics is 3.
