# Homotopy category

The **naïve homotopy category** $\Top / {\simeq}$ is a [[quotient category]] built from [[Category of topological spaces]] where objects remain [[Topological space|topological spaces]] but hom-sets are homotopy quotients,
i.e. morphisms are not continuous functions but [[Homotopy of continuous maps|homotopy classes]] of continuous functions.
Topological spaces that are isomorphic in $\Top / {\simeq}$ are called (strongly) [[Homotopy equivalence|homotopy equivalent]].
