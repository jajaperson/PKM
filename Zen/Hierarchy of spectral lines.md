# Hierarchy of spectral lines

Central to atomic physics are the various levels of resolution in the energy levels of an atom.


| structure level         | energy (eV)         | frequency (Hz)                | atomic effects                                                                    |
| ----------------------- | ------------------- | ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Gross structure]]     | $1{-}10$            | $10^{15}{-}10^{16}$ (visible) | electron-nucleus attraction, electron kinetic energy, electron-electron repulsion |
| [[Fine structure]]      | $10^{-3}{-}10^{-2}$ | $10^{11}{-}10^{12}$ (far-IR)  | spin-orbit coupling, relativistic corrections                                     |
| [[Hyperfine structure]] | $10^{-6}{-}10^{-5}$ | $10^8{-}10^9$ (microwave)     | nucleus-electron electromagnetic interaction                                      |

See also [[Spectral lines in astronomy]]
