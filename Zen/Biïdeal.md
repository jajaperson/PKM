# Biïdeal

Let $\mathcal{B}$ be a [[R-bimonoid]].
A <dfn>biïdeal</dfn> $I \leq \mathcal{B}$ is at once a (two-sided) [[algebra ideal|ideal]] and [[coïdeal]].

- The [[Linear kernel|kernel]] of a $\mathcal{K}$-[[bimonoid morphism]] is a biïdeal.
- Given a biïdeal $I$ one may construct a [[Quotient R-bimonoid|quotient $\mathcal K$-bimonoid]] $\mathcal{B} / I$, giving the converse of the above.

## See also

- If $\mathcal{B}$ is a [[Hopf algebra]], there are [[Hopf ideal#Theorem|cases]] where biïdeal coïncides with [[Hopf ideal]].
