# Elementary topos

An <dfn>elementary topos</dfn> is a [[category]] $\cat E$ such that[^1994] #m/def/topos

1. $\cat E$ is [[Completeness and cocompleteness|finitely bicomplete]], that is it has all finitary [[Products and coproducts|products and coproducts]];
2. $\cat E$ has all [[exponential object|exponentials]];
3. $\cat{E}$ has a [[subobject classifier]] $\mathrm{tt} : 1 \to \Omega$.

[^1994]: 1994\. [[Sources/@maclaneSheavesGeometryLogic1994|Sheaves in geometry and logic: A first introduction to topos theory]], p. 48
