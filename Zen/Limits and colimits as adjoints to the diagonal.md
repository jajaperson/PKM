[[Limits and colimits]]
# Limits and colimits as adjoints to the diagonal

Let $\cat C, \cat J$ be categories with $\cat J$ small, and consider the [[diagonal functor]]
$$
\begin{align*}
\Delta_{\cat J} : \cat C \to \cat C^{\cat J}
\end{align*}
$$
from $\cat C$ to its [[Commutative diagram|category of diagrams]].
Then the [[Adjoint functor|left and right adjoints]] of $\Delta_{\cat J}$, if they exist are given by [[Limits and colimits|colimits and limits]] respectively.[^2010]
$$
\begin{align*}
\colim_{\cat J} \dashv \Delta_{\cat J} \dashv \catlim _{\cat J}
\end{align*}
$$
