[[Bimonoid object]]
# Bimonoid morphism

Given [[Bimonoid object|bimonoids]] $B, B'$ in $\cat C$, a morphism $f : B \to B'$ is a <dfn>bimonoid morphism</dfn> iff it is both a [[monoid morphism]] and [[comonoid morphism]] of the underlying (co)monoids. #m/def/cat
These form the morphisms in the [[Category of bimonoid objects]].
