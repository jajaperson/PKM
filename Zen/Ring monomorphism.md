# Ring monomorphism

Let $\varphi : R \to S$ be a [[ring homomorphism]].
The following statements are equivalent:[^2009] #m/thm/ring 

- $\varphi$ is injective;
- $\varphi$ is [[monic]];
- $\ker \varphi = 0$
