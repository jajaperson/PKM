# Subobject

A subgroup of an object in a category $\cat C$ generalizes the idea of [[subset]], [[subgroup]], [[vector subspace]], and more.
Formally, a <dfn>subobject</dfn> of an object $X \in \cat C$ is an [[isomorphism class]] of [[monomorphism|monomorphisms]] [[Overcategory|over]] $X$. #m/def/cat
