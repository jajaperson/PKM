# Functional analysis MOC

**Functional analysis** extends and specialized concepts from [[linear algebra MOC]] to infinite-dimensional vector spaces, in particular [[function space|function spaces]] and [[Sequence space|sequence spaces]].
These vector spaces are endowed with limit structure, e.g. [[topological vector space|TVSs]], [[Normed vector space|normed spaces]], and [[Inner product space|inner product spaces]].
Often we want [[Complete metric space|completeness]], as guaranteed by [[Banach space|Banach spaces]] and [[Hilbert space|Hilbert spaces]].
