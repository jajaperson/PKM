# Projective space

An $n$-dimensional **projective space** over a field $\mathbb{K}$ refers to an extension of $n$-dimensional affine space such that parallel lines intersect at infinity.
This notion is axiomatized by the [[abstract projective space]].
One way of constructing this is a [[Projectivization]] of a vector space,
which is the space of 1-dimensional subspaces (i.e. lines intersecting the origin) of the [[vector space]] $\mathbb{K}^{n+1}$.
