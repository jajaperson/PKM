# Linear monomorphism

Let $T: U \to V$ be a $\mathbb{K}$-[[linear map]].
The following statements are equivalent:

- $T$ is [[Surjectivity, injectivity, and bijectivity|injective]];
- $T$ is [[Morphism|monic]] in [[Category of vector spaces]];
- $T$ is [[Split monomorphism|split monic]] in [[Category of vector spaces]];
- $\ker T = \{0 \}$.

> [!check]- Proof
> See [[Module monomorphism]]
