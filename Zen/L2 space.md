[[Lebesgue space]]
# $L^2$ space

Let $(X, \Sigma, \mu)$ be a [[measure space]].
The [[Lebesgue space]] $L^2(X,\mu)$ of complex-valued [[Measurable function|measurable]] and square-integrable functions[^quot] becomes a [[Hilbert space]] with the inner product
$$
\begin{align*}
\braket{ f | g } = \int _{X} f(x)^* g(x) \, d\mu(x) 
\end{align*}
$$

[^quot]: [[Algebraic quotient|modulo]] functions that are equal [[almost everywhere]].
