[[Category theory MOC]]
# Univalent category

A <dfn>univalent category</dfn>[^1] $\cat C_{0}$ is a category such that the canonical map
$$
\begin{align*}
\mathrm{idtoiso}_{\cat C} : (A \equiv_{\cat C_{0}} B) \to (A \cong_{\cat C} B)
\end{align*}
$$
is an [[Typal equivalence|equivalence]].
Such categories make it impossible to disrespect the [[principle of equivalence]].
