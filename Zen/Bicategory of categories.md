# Bicategory of categories

The <dfn>bicategory of categories</dfn> $\mathfrak{Cat}$ is a [[bicategory]] where an object is ([[Small collection|small]]) [[category]] and the hom-category $\mathfrak{Cat}(\cat A, \cat B)$ is the [[functor category]] $\cat B^{\cat A}$. #m/def/cat 
The composition bifunctor is given by ordinary composition of functors, with all coherence conditions holding definitionally.
