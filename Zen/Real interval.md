[[Real numbers]]
# Real interval

The <dfn>real interval</dfn> $\mathbb{I} \sube \mathbb{R}$ is the subset defined by #m/def/anal 
$$
\begin{align*}
\mathbb{I} := [0,1] = \{ x \in \mathbb{R} :\mid: 0 \leq x \leq 1 \}.
\end{align*}
$$
usually regarded as a [[topological space]] under the [[subspace topology]].
