# Category of presheaves

The **category of presheaves** $\PSh(\cat C) := \Set^{\op{\cat C}}$ is a [[category]] where
an object is a [[Presheaf]]
and a morphism is a [[natural transformation]]. #m/def/cat 
Thus it is a particular example of a [[functor category]].

## Properties

- By the [[Yoneda embedding]], $\cat C$ is a [[Subcategory|full subcategory]] of $\PSh(\cat C)$.

## Examples

Many categories of interest are conveniently expressed as the presheaves of a “presenting category.”

- [[Category of quivers]]
- [[Category of simplicial sets]]
