# Basin of attraction

The **basin of attraction** $B$ of a fixed point $\mathbf{x}^*$ is the set of all points $\mathbf{x} \in M$ such that $\lim_{ t \to \infty } \Phi^t \mathbf{x} = \mathbf{x}^*$.

## Properties

- A manifold between two basins of attraction is a **separatrix**
