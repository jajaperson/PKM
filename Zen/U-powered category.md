[[Category theory MOC]]
# $\mathcal{U}$-powered category

Given a universe $\mathcal{U}$, a category $\cat C$ is called <dfn>$\mathcal{U}$-powered</dfn> iff i.e. every object $x \in \cat C$ has a [[poset]] of [[subobject|subobjects]] which is equivalent to one which is $\mathcal{U}$-small, #m/def/cat i.e. [[Displayed category of subobjects]] is equivalent to a $\mathcal{U}$-small displayed category.
