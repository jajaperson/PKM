[[Finite ring]]
# Wedderburn's little theorem

Suppose $R$ is a [[Finite ring]].
Then the following are equivalent:

1. $R$ is an [[integral domain]];
2. $R$ is a [[division ring]];
3. $R$ is a [[Galois field]].
