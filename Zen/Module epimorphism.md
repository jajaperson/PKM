# Module epimorphism

Let $f : M \to N$ be an $R$-[[module homomorphism]].
The following statements are equivalent:

1. $f$ is [[Surjectivity, injectivity, and bijectivity|surjective]].
2. $f$ is an [[epimorphism]] in [[Category of left modules]].
3. $\coker f = 0$.
4. $f$ is a [[regular epimorphism]].
5. (with [[Axiom of Choice|AC]]) $f$ is a [[split epimorphism]] in $\lMod R$.
