[[Phonetics and Phonology MOC]]
# Breathy voice
**Breathy voice**, denoted by `◌ʱ`  in the [[International Phonetic Alphabet]],
is a combination of voicing and aspiration that resembles a sigh.

## [[Phonologically contrastive]] occurrence
- [[Indo-Aryan languages]] 
  — four-way contrast in [[Plosive|plosives]] and [[Affricate|affricates]]
- [[Nguni languages]]
  — the [[Xhosa language]], for example,
  has a four-way distinction for [[Oral click|oral clicks]]
  and a three-way distinction for [[Plosive|plosives]].
