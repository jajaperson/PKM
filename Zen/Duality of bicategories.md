# Duality of 2-categories

There are three different ways to generalize [[opposite category]] to a [[bicategory]] $\mathfrak{C}$: #m/def/cat/bi

1. The <dfn>1-morphism dual</dfn> $\mathfrak{C}^\mathrm{op}$, which has 1-morphisms reversed; ^1
2. The <dfn>2-morphism dual</dfn> $\mathfrak{C}^\mathrm{co}$, which has 2-morphisms reversed; ^2
3. The <dfn>$\{1,2\}$-morphism dual</dfn> $\mathfrak{C}^\mathrm{coop}$, which has all morphisms reversed. ^1-2
