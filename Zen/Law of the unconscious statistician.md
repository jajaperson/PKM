# Law of the unconscious statistician

The **law of the unconscious statistician** or **LOTUS** states the [[Expectation]] of a [[random function]].
In the discrete case,
$$
\begin{align*}
\Ex[g(X)] = \sum_{x \in \mathrm{supp}(X)}g(x) \,\mathbb{P}(X=x)
\end{align*}
$$
and in the continuous case
$$
\begin{align*}
\Ex[g(X)] = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} g(x) \, d\mathbb{P}(X=x) = \int_{-\infty }^{\infty} g(x)\,f_{X}(x) \, dx 
\end{align*}
$$
