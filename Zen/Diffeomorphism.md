[[Differential geometry MOC]]
# Diffeomorphism

A $C^\alpha$-**diffeomorphism** is an [[Morphism|isomorphism]] of [[Differentiable manifold|differentiable manifolds]], i.e. in [[Category of manifolds]].

## Further terminology

- Given a manifold $M$, we call $\Aut^\alpha (M) := \Aut_{\Man^\alpha}(M)$ its **group of diffeomorphisms**.
