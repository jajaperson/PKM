[[Contravariant functor]]
# Presheaf

A **presheaf** $F$ on a category $\cat C$ is a [[contravariant functor]] $F : \op{\cat C} \to \Set$, #m/def/cat 
and the corresponding [[functor category]] is called [[Category of presheaves|$\PSh(\cat C)$]].
