# Kronecker category

The $n$-<dfn>Kronecker category</dfn> $\underline{\Theta_{n}}$ is the [[free category]] on the $n$-[[Kronecker quiver]] $\Theta_{n}$. #m/def/cat 
Thus it has no nontrivial endomorphisms and
$$
\begin{align*}
\Ob(\underline{\Theta_{n}}) &= [2] = \{0,1\},
&
\underline{\Theta_{n}}(0,1) &= [n].
\end{align*}
$$

## Properties

- Swapping $0$ and $1$ and fixing morphism gives an antiautoëquivalence, thus $\underline{\Theta_{n}}$ is self-dual.
