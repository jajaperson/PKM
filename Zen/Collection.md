[[Foundation-agnostic mathematics]]
# Collection

<dfn>Collection</dfn> is an informal notion used in these notes for [[Foundation-agnostic mathematics]], which may correspond to

- A [[class]],
- A [[set]] of any size,
- A [[Type theory|type]] of any [[h-level]].

Notably, we do not require a collection to have any notion of equality on its elements.
