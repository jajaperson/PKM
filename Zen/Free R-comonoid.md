# Free $\mathcal{K}$-comonoid

Let $S$ be a inhabited set.
The **free $\mathcal{K}$-comonoid** $\mathcal{K}^{(S)}$ is the corresponding [[free module]] with the additional structure of an [[R-comonoid]] given by
$$
\begin{align*}
\Delta (s) &= s \otimes s, & \epsilon (s) = 1
\end{align*}
$$
for all $s \in S$.
