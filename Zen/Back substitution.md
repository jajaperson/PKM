# Back substitution
Back substitution is a simple process by which a system of linear equations in [[row echelon form]] may be solved.
Starting from the last row and working upwards,
every [[Basic and free variables|basic variable]] is expressed as a combination of  [[Basic and free variables|free variables]] and constants.
