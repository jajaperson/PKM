# Small collection

The precise meaning of **small** depends on the choice of [[Foundation of mathematics|foundation]] being used.

- In [[ZFC]] and its [[conservative extension|conservative extensions]] (e.g. [[NBG]]),
    the term merely emphasizes that a [[set]] is not a [[proper class]].
- In foundations with universes such as [[TG]], a set $x$ is **small** with respect to a universe $U$ if $x \in U$.
