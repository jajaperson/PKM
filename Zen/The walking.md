# The walking gadget

**The walking** gadget is a phrase indicating some kind of initial exemplar of a gadget,
so that gadgets can be identified with morphisms or functors from the walking gadget.
This is best demonstrated by example:

- The category [[Interval category]] is the walking morphism;
- The category [[Ordinal category|$\underline 3$]] is the walking composition.
