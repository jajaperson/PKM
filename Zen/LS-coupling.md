[[Spin-orbit coupling]]
# $LS$-coupling

**$LS$-coupling** or **Russel-Saunders coupling** is the effect of [[Spin-orbit coupling]] in the regime where $H_{\mathrm{re}} \gg H_{\mathrm{so}}$, 
and hence total $L$ and $S$ couple as $J$.
This approximation is most correct for atoms with a small to medium atomic numbers.

The resulting energy level is described by [[Russel-Saunders term symbol]] (or rather, a level symbol).
