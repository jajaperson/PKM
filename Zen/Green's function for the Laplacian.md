# Green's function for the Laplacian
A [[Green's function]] for the [[Laplacian]] is given by
$$
\begin{align*}
G(\vab r - \vab r') &= -\frac{1}{4\pi \Sr}
\end{align*}
$$
where $\Sr = \|\vab r - \vab r'\|$
which satisfies
$$
\begin{align*}
\nabla^2 G(\vab r - \vab r') &= \delta^3(\vab r - \vab r')
\end{align*}
$$
