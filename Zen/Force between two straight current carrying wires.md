# Force between two straight current carrying wires
Given two long straight conductors, combining the equations for [[Magnetic force on a wire]] and [[Magnetic field from a long straight wire]] gives
$$
\begin{align*}
\abs{\vab F_{\to2}} &= I_{2}\abs{\vab \ell \times \vab B_{1}} \\
                 &= I_{2}\ell \frac{\mu_{0}I_{1}}{2\pi d} \\
                 &= \frac{\mu_{0}I_{1}I_{2}\ell}{2\pi d}     
\end{align*}
$$
