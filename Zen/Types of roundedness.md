# Types of roundedness
There are two main types of rounding:

1. **Protrusive** — 
   With protruding, tube-like lips.
   Typically represented as ‹◌ʷ›.
2. **Compressed** — 
   With the corners of the mouth horizontally draw together.
   Typically represented as ‹◌ᵝ›.

Note the transcription methods outlined above are not part of the IPA standard.
