# Signed group

A <dfn>signed group</dfn> $(G,-1)$ is a [[group]] with a distinguished [[Centre of a group|central]] involution $-1 \in \opn Z(G)$.[^2025] #m/def/group/super 

[^2025]: 2025\. [[Sources/@craigenSignedGroupsBasic2025|Signed groups I: Basic theory, application to orthogonal matrices]]

## Further terminology

- The [[quotient group]] $\opn P(G) := G / \{ \pm 1 \}$ is called the <dfn>projection group</dfn>.
