# Hopf theory MOC

By “<dfn>Hopf theory</dfn>”[^1] I mean the study of [[Hopf algebra|Hopf algebras]] and related constructions in the monoidal category [[Category of modules over a commutative ring]].[^2]
As such, the notation of [[Single faced string diagram|string diagrams]] is useful,
both for giving proofs and building intuition.
