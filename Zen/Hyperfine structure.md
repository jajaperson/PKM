[[Hierarchy of spectral lines]]
# Hyperfine structure

**Hyperfine structure** refers to the energy level splitting caused by electromagnetic multipole interactions between the nucleus and electron cloud.
This splitting is to the order of a microëlectronvolt and transitions between hyperfine levels produces radiation in the microwave range 
— around $\frac{m_{e}}{m_{p}}$ times smaller than [[Fine structure]].

At this resolution we introduce nuclear spin $\mathbf{I}$ and grand total angular momentum $\mathbf{F} = \mathbf{I} + \mathbf{J}$.
