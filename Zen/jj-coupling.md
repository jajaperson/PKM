[[Spin-orbit coupling]]
# jj-coupling

**$jj$-coupling** is the effect of [[Spin-orbit coupling]] in the regime where the spin-orbit hamiltonian is at least as large as the spin-spin hamiltonian,
and hence individual $j$ couple to $J$.
This approximation is most correct for atoms with large atomic numbers.
