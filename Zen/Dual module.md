# Dual module

Let $V$ be a left $R$-[[module]].
The <dfn>dual module</dfn> $V^*$ is the space of all linear functions $V \to R$,
i.e. as a set $V^* = \lMod{R}(V,R)$. #m/def/module 
The additional and scalar multiplication are pointwise.
Sometimes we denote evaluation by
$$
\begin{align*}
\langle f, v \rangle = f(v)
\end{align*}
$$
for $f \in V^*$, $v \in V$.
