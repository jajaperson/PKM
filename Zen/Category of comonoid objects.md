# Category of comonoid objects

The **category of comonoids in $\cat C$**, denoted $\cat{Comon}_{\cat C}$ is a [[category]] where
an object is a [[comonoid object]] in $\cat C$
and a morphism is a [[Homomorphism of comonoid objects]]. #m/def/cat

## As the cartesian completion of a symmetric monoidal category

From the [[Category of monoid objects#As the cocartesian completion of a symmetric monoidal category|dual result]], it immediately follows that if $\cat C$ is a [[symmetric monoidal category]],
then $\cat{Comon}_{\cat C}$ is a [[cocartesian category]] under the natural tensor product. #m/thm/cat
