# Local Lagrangian

A **local Lagrangian** of order $k$
on some space $F$ of smooth maps from $M$
is a [[Action functional|Lagrangian]] $L : F\to \Omega^m (M)$ depending smoothly only on the base point $x \in M$ and partial derivatives of its argument up to order $k$. #m/def/geo/diff
