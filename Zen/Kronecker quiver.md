[[Quiver representation]]
# Kronecker quiver

Let $n \in \mathbb{N}$.
The $n$-<dfn>Kronecker quiver</dfn> $\Theta_{n}$ is a [[quiver]] with vertices $\{ 0, 1 \}$
and precisely $n$ edges $0 \to 1$, #m/def/quiv 
i.e.
$$
\begin{align*}
(\Theta_{n})_{0} &= [2],
&
\Theta_{n}(0,1) &= [n].
\end{align*}
$$
The corresponding [[free category]] $\underline{\Theta_{n}}$ is called the $n$-[[Kronecker category]].
