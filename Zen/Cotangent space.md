[[Differential geometry MOC]]
# Cotangent space

The **cotangent space** $T_{p}^*M$ f a [[differentiable manifold]] $M$ at a point $p \in M$ is the dual of the [[tangent space]], i.e. it consists of linear functionals on the tangent space. #m/def/geo/diff 
$$
\begin{align*}
T_{p}^* M = (T_{p}M)^*
\end{align*}
$$
However, for various reasons one might want to define the cotangent space independently (which enables defining the tangent space as the dual of the cotangent space), as given below.
See also [[Cotangent bundle]].

## Alternative definition

Let $p \in M$ be a point, and consider the [[ideal]] $I_{p} \trianglelefteq C^\alpha(M)$ of functions vanishing at $p$.
Then
$$
\begin{align*}
T_{p}^* M := I_{p} / I_{p}^2
\end{align*}
$$
