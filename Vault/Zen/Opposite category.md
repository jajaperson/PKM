# Opposite category

Let $\cat C$ be a [[category]].
The **opposite category** $\cop C$ is the category with the same objects as $\cat C$ but whose morphisms have the domain and codomain swapped, #m/def/cat 
i.e.
$$
\begin{align*}
f \in \cat C(X,Y) \iff f \in \cop C (Y,X)
\end{align*}
$$
