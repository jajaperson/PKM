[[Algebra over a field]]
# Subalgebra

A **subalgebra** $W$ of a [[Algebra over a field|algebra]] $(V, \cdot)$ is a [[vector subspace]] $W \sube V$ that is closed under $(\cdot)$, #m/def/falg 
i.e. $W\cdot W \sube W$.

## Specifications

- [[Lie subalgebra]]
- [[Unital subalgebra]] also demands the identity be included
