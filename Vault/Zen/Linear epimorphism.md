# Linear epimorphism

Let $T : U \to V$ be a $\mathbb{K}$-[[linear map]].
The following statements are equivalent: #m/thm/linalg 

- $T$ is [[Surjectivity, injectivity, and bijectivity|surjective]]
- $T$ is [[Morphism|epic]] in [[Category of vector spaces]]
