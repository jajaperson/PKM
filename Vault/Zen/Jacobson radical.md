# Jacobson radical

Let $R$ be a [[ring]].
The **Jacobson radical** $J(R)$ is defined as the intersection of the [[Annihilator|annihilators]] of all simple modules #m/def/ring 
$$
\begin{align*}
J(R) = \bigcap_{\text{$M$ simple}} \lAnn R M
\end{align*}
$$

and is thus a two-sided ideal.
