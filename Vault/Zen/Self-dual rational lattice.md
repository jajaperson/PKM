[[Rational lattice]]
# Self-dual rational lattice

A [[rational lattice]] is **self-dual** iff it is its own [[Dual of a rational lattice|dual lattice]], or equivalently it is [[Rational lattice#^integral]] and [[Rational lattice#^unimodular]].
