[[Ring]]
# Finite ring

A **finite ring** $R$ is a [[ring]] with finitely many elements. #m/def/ring

## Properties

- [[Wedderburn's little theorem]] implies $R$ is an [[integral domain]] iff it is a [[division ring]] iff it is a [[Galois field]].
