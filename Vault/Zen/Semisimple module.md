# Semisimple module

A (left) [[module]] $M \in \lMod R$ is (left) **semisimple** or **completely reducible** iff it is the [[Direct sum of modules|direct sum]] of [[Simple module|simple]] [[Submodule|submodules]]. #m/def/module
