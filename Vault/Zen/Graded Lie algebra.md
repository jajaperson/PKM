# Graded Lie algebra
Let $(\mathfrak{A}, +)$ be a (necessarily abelian) [[monoid]].
A $\mathfrak{A}$-**graded Lie algebra** is an example of a [[graded algebra]], #m/def/lie
i.e.
$$
\begin{align*}
[A_{\alpha}, A_{\beta}] \sube A_{\alpha + \beta}
\end{align*}
$$
