# Grassmannian

The **Grassmannian** $\opn{Gr}_{k}(V)$ is the space of $k$-dimensional [[Vector subspace|subspaces]] of a vector space $V$ over a field $\mathbb{K}$.
It is thus a generalization of the [[Projectivization]] $\opn{P}(V)=\opn{Gr}_{1}(V)$.
