[[Real random variable]]
# Location-scale transformation

Let $X$ be a [[real random variable]] and $Y = \sigma X + \mu$.
Then we say that $Y$ is a [[real random variable]] given by a l**ocation-scale transformation** of $X$. #m/def/prob
