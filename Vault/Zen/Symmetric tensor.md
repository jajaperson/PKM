# Symmetric tensor

A **symmetric tensor** is a (unmixed contravariant) [[Tensor]] $S \in V^{\otimes n}$ that is invariant under the action of [[Symmetric group|$S_n$]] on $V^{\otimes n}$ given by permutation of arguments. #m/def/linalg/multi
